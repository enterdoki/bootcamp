Who We Are

We are a group of students who are tired of sitting on the wayside while our planet is being scorched by big corporations for profit. Our law makers are deep in their pockets and has incentive to pass laws that benefit their corporate overlords. We are tired of it. Students For Change is an organization where students of all ages in all corners of the world to congregate so that our voices are heard. We advocate climate awareness, dispell misinformation about climate change, and organize protests. Tackling climate change is no easy task, but humans are capabale of great things and saving the planet is one of them.

What You Can Do

Educate yourself about climate change through trusted sources on our resources page. If you like the things we are doing, consider going to our donations page and give some money for our cause. Most importantly, share this webpage with your friends. Plant some trees together, protest together, or clean up some beaches together. Saving the planet is a collective effort, and together we can stop climate change and preserve our only home for future generations.
